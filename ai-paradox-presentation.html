03 — Trade-Offs

The Sharing Economy
Hits Intellectual Property

✦ Pros

    • Rapid innovation through community iteration and public benchmarks
    • Lower barrier for academic research and emerging markets
    • Transparency in model behavior and training methodology
    • Enables specialized fine-tuning without data privacy risks
    • Reduces dependency on single-vendor infrastructure

✦ Cons

    • Massive capital expenditure with no direct revenue model
    • Adversarial fine-tuning and safety alignment bypasses
    • Fragmentation of the ecosystem across incompatible formats
    • Compute cost at scale remains prohibitive without cloud
    • Liability gap in enterprise deployment and compliance
